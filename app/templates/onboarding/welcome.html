Let's Get You Set Up for Success

To match you with the best funding opportunities, we'll gather some key information about your organization. The more complete your profile, the better our AI can identify grants that align with your mission.

Your Onboarding Journey

5-7 minutes
  • 1
    Basic Information (2 min)
    Organization details, legal status, and contact information
  • 2
    Mission & Programs (3 min)
    Your purpose, focus areas, and the communities you serve
  • 3
    Capacity & Impact (2 min)
    Organization size, budget, and key achievements

What You'll Need

✓ Your organization's legal name
✓ EIN/Tax ID (helpful but optional)
✓ Mission statement
✓ Primary focus areas
✓ Annual budget range
✓ Number of staff/volunteers
✓ Geographic service area
✓ Key programs or services
